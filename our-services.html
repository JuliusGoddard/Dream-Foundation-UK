Our Services

Donate

Through fundraising efforts in the UK, Dream Foundation UK will award grants to select partner organisations (registered charities and organisations) in the defined areas (KENYA, UGANDA, TANZANIA & INDIA) for the procurement of essential items and services for the benefit of people going through financial hardship/poverty.

Working with select partner organisations will enable Dream Foundation UK to provide grants for the prevention & relief of poverty. The current situation in these defined locations demonstrates the devasting impact of poverty on the general population, with children and young people bearing the brunt of this hardship.

The grants that Dream Foundation UK provides will range from £20 - £2000 which will be delivered directly to our selected partner organisations and/or team of trusted volunteers who will procure the items and distribute them accordingly to disadvantaged individuals experiencing poverty. The grants provided are utilised for purchasing essential items, services and facilities that beneficiaries would otherwise be unable to afford. Examples include grants for the provision of food parcels and assistance; access to health supplies and treatment, payment of residential housing.
