Our Mission

At Dream Foundation UK, our mission is to make a positive difference in the lives of vulnerable children and young people. As trustees, we are fully committed to providing opportunities and support that can help create a brighter future for those we assist. We take a holistic approach to our work, taking into account the social, emotional, and psychological needs of the individuals we serve. Our aim is to empower these young people and provide a safe haven where they feel valued, respected and supported by a community that cares.
